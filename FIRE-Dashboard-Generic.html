Social Security — Realistic Estimator & Three-Phase FIRE

SS benefits are based on your highest 35 years of earnings. With only a few years of US work history, most of those 35 slots are $0 — drastically reducing your real benefit. Your FIRE has three phases: FIRE→59.5 (taxable only) → 59.5→SS (401K unlocks) → SS onward.
